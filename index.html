Our Process to Change the world.

Our revolutionary robotic designing begins and ends with our teams. Each one plays an integral role in the final product, and as such, each is worthy of its own spot. The product being marketed here is derivative of the cohesion of the efforts of each team. No one team is more important than the others, and it is through this unparallelled level of cooperation that the final result is achieved.

Marketing.

The marketing team was tasked with spreading the word of our designs throughout the budding robotic-aided farming market. This was achieved through candid meetings between the marketing team and local leaders of the agricultural industry. This partnership between our team and industry was integral to the success of the team.

Design.

The design team was tasked with giving the construction team a series of designs spanning from the booth to the robot itself. The majority of this was accomplished by applying the industry standards of 3-Dimensional AutoCAD software. Thus, after a multitude of analysis' of each structure, the build team could rest assured that the creations they were building would have no trouble withstanding the pressure it would be inevitbaly placed under.

Construction.

The construction team's singular focus was the creation of the aparatures that make up the product. Anything from a booth design, to the robot itself, the build team was more than happy to oblige. Using cutting edge industrial standards, the construction team surpassed even the expectations of the entire team.

Graphic Arts.

The graphic arts department was tasked with the creation of anything requiring the skills derived from the afore-mentioned major. Everything from banners, to websites coding and creation, to font-facing; all were challenges met by the graphic arts department.

Presentation Team

The presentation team is the face of Yellow Jacket Industries. This group represents the full span of the team, as representatives from all around the organization are brought together to not only head up the communications between groups, but further, to eloquently translate the work of each group into a finalized product.

Programming Team.

The programming team's singular focus was supplying the design and build team with programs which would successfully result in a mobile and functional robot.

Who We Are.

Robotics is ultimately the technology of the future. In this ever-changing world, the need for a skilled workforce prepared to institute change is only growing. That is where we come in. We are an organization whose primary focus is the creation of robotic innovation. Every day, we bring ourselves closer to "the next big thing" in robotics, all the while, acquiring the skills, connections, and comraderie that are so pivotal to any successful team.

Our Process.

We tackle every challenge with our three step method. First, we recognize an issue, and all derived factors. Next, we initialize the process to find the solution. In order to do this, we generally impliment the cutting edge vocational standards. These include the latest tools, software, and technology. Finally, we divide and conquer using an intuitive sorting method which permits us to each focus on our strengths, while strengthening our weaknesses.

Our Approach.

Our approach is an unorthodox method fitting of innovation. We employ socratic seminars to discuss progress, and plan for the future. All the while, theory is turned into reality through second-by-second prototyping and AutoCAD based simulations. This culminates into an unparalleled cooperation of students whom, despite having a number of varying skills, cohesively create finished products, fit to bear the name, Yellow Jacket Industries.

Our Goal.

Our goal is to bring robotics to the forefront of public awareness, and help the world understand just how important robotics is to our very future.

Our Mission.

Here at Yellow Jacket Industries, we endeavor to help change the world by not only creating amazing robots, but also spreading the word about the possibilities robotics brings to the table.
